Caged


i'm skeptical of the cage of poetry...

it tricks me into shoving words up my own ass and becoming a puppet


i'm skeptical of becoming a poet...

i oft don't even really know what words are so just fondle about until i find some connection

to another word or person or idea and then mis-mangle those gems into a story or a message

or a lesson overexplained.. is that all poetry is or perhaps that’s just me

 

a poem when a glance would do

a poem when a sigh is enough

a poem when math and art and music exist

 

i'm a hypocrite...

that needs no extrapolation

 

i am skeptical of poets

diminuendo

φ

elif

limiting vocabulary to spoken words

limiting ideas to spoken language

 

i am skeptical of poetry

chaos and disorder, form and verse and so caged in...

nowhere to go except the human voice and i fold when i read

so this art remains trapped on a page forever silent

at least a cage is 3-D... does my poem think its Trompe-l'œil

 

And here i sit writing – saddened, excited, lost, caged

 

i’m skeptical of poetry...

i could do without it all if it would kindly just leave me with the poems
