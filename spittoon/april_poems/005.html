My City

 

There was nowhere else I could end up

Sure, it was more chance than anything

But from Big Sky to 天府 makes sense

And Andy and 安迪 have the same ring

 

From the frozen plains of Montana

Bounced around not stirring up too much

A few broken bones – a lot of stories

Fountain diving, park wrestling, and such

 

Now this town-kid, country-kid cowboy

Sits up on the forty-third floor

Settled into a view as far as he can see

The city cascades – he wants nothing more

 

There was nowhere else I could end up

The food, the vibes, the people, the stride

It all feels so comfortable to me

Allowing me to be just along for the ride

 

So now I have a state and a city

Pride in each – not lost, not empty

A town-kid cowboy on the 43rd floor

Waxing poetic about Chengdu and MT
