Beautiful Day

 

“What a beautiful day, today, eh?“

 

Not today, man, just leave me be

 

“Sure, yeah,“ I manage

 

I hustle past pretending to be late – gesturing down the hall

 

Am I the grump of the office? – The quiet, irritable sod

No, I put on a smile – or at least a subtle nod

 

A quick glance out the window – well, he isn’t wrong

Up five flights of stairs – walking up sand dunes

 

Get it together – you’re here, you showed up

You have shit to take care of – just keep walking

No reward – this IS the reward – be here

You can handle today if you’re not the one talking

 

Busy enough to forget the weight – long enough to find a real smile

And then a moment of rest and my mind starts to drift awhile

 

“A beautiful day” shouldn’t irk me so

Maybe it’s that it’s so easy for others

I know everyone fights their battles

But...

 

i don't know if the demons i fight most days could swallow your demons whole

but then again

i don't know if the demons i fight are as big as the ones i let in the back door

 

Forget this uselessness – or write it down and move on

 

Words on the page – eyes out the window

 

I mean he wasn’t wrong – grateful for those who point out beautiful days
