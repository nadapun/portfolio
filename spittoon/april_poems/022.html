Why I Absolutely Deserve You

 

The sunlight wakes me

You beside me – aware yet?

I lie awhile – to myself

I absolutely deserve you

 

Twisted sheets – lack of sleep

A story of a night I shouldn't tell

And here we are wrapped together

With what conviction, I know not

I pull myself up - slippers on

Brush my teeth - make breakfast

I wonder if I really deserve you

 

You salter in - back to bed?

But, today I need to be a person

I made breakfast instead?

Your weight on my shoulders

A presence I accept with grace

 

Some more time with me in bed

Today can wait - What's the harm?

You’re right - had our fun - rest now

Back in bed - not setting alarms

I absolutely deserve you

 

Wake with a start - check the clock

Half the day gone - I had plans

I almost forget you're there but then

The day's gone, stay here, you’re right

Did you or I come to this conclusion

 

I need to shower and at least

Conquer one task - one beast

Your arms hold me down - no

I welcome the embrace - the excuse

I need you - a reason to stay

The sins of last night belayed

 

The same sun that woke me falls

And I have done nothing but

Lay with you, your poignant thoughts

Your weight - your reasons to stay

I absolutely deserve you

 

We fall asleep having done nothing

And maybe that's okay - a day

with you is a day I must endure

 

For tomorrow I wake to my alarm

You're still here - but your weight

Today, I can manage your figure

Reduced to an imp, waking up yourself

And I let you crawl on my shoulders

 

I shower and brush my teeth

You cling to me muttering something

I'm out the door, you perched

On my shoulder - never gone

 

I need you - you're my excuse

Why I don't show up - why I can fail - why I become angry sometimes

And so I wear you another day

And perhaps later I'll feed you

And we shall lay in tangled sheets

I absolutely deserve you - because I feed you
