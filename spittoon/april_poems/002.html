Different Shapes

 

Mono-monostatic

Only one true resting point

Weebles wobble but

Gömböcs do too

 

Bouncing from idea to idea

Learning constantly

Shifting – changing

I try to bee a different person every day

 

Don’t bee a gömböc

Don’t bee mono-monostatic

Don’t return to the same tired ideas

Don’t bee afraid of different shapes

 

Change yourself

Create – dance – I don’t care

Move and while you do make sure

You don’t return to the same resting point – you don’t settle into a groove – a comfort

 

Don’t bee afraid of circles – of π

Don’t bee afraid of spirals – of φ

Don’t bee afraid of trying new things – learning new things – beeing new things – breaking the rules – changing shapes

Don’t bee a gömböc, my friends

 

What happens when an idea pushes you?

Do you fall back in place?

Will you wake up in a different resting place tomorrow?

Will you wake up a different shape tomorrow?
