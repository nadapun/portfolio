Phenomenologically

 

Pace has been walking

Beside me this week

Almost mocking me

Too slow, too busy

 

Fuck Pace – the bus bending around the corner

I’m always behind looking at rides I’m not gonna catch

Fuck Pace – the bus bending around the corner 

 

And when I finally sit

Regain m’breath a bit

Off he goes – perhaps

He has another mark   

 

Fuck Pace – the bus bending around the corner

Not giving me a look inside this week – no lesson

Fuck Pace – the bus bending around the corner

 

And so, I sit on a bench

Searching for a metaphor

Fuck Pace, already gone

Madly staring skyward and I hear a voice – one I’m always happy to hear

 

Scale - that beautiful fun-house mirror

Come to impart a lesson or some calm?

Scale - that beautiful fun-house mirror

 

She speaks to me

Off a bus stop’s wall

Glass reflecting a sky

A figure, a building

 

And the world slows down

You hate Pace when he runs

You hate him more

When he sticks around   

 

I know not my size

But Scale, she pokes

and prods and lets me know

What and who I am – if only for a moment

 

Scale – that beautiful fun-house mirror

She is why men climb mountains and fight wars

She is the cause of pride and light – insight  

But she rarely reveals the secrets people seek

 

She’s why I draw and drink and travel and dance

Hoping I might run into her on the streets

Scale – that beautiful fun-house mirror –

Wishing we could sit as she whispers in my ear

 

 

So, I sit and listen

No worldly secrets

But a reshaping of me

Perhaps a quiet calm

 

He meant no ill harm

He is here to help

Buses have numbers

On front and back

Not to mock us

But to help us prepare

The next bus is coming

Will you get on?

 

I was wrong

 

Not in the mood,

Not for wisdom, today,

Scale - let me pout

I’m tired and have more to do and fuck Pace

 

Pace - the bus bending around the corner

Why are we even friends?  I just want some sleep

 

Scale – that beautiful fun house mirror

Wanders away disappointed in me

 

She gave me a secret – which she rarely does

And I had the gall to spurn her advice

 

I wonder how long ‘til I see her again.

 

So, there I sit

Feeling small

Feeling behind

Feeling unknown

 

When the third phenomenologically poking and prodding and pushing and lighting- things-on-fire fiend rears his fiery head

 

It makes sense today

 

I’m so full of irreverence yet stuttering

Need Pain to

Whisper in m’ear her effervescent muttering

 

So squat next to me

I see a haunting form

And I forget all

I’m not fast or slow

I’m not big or small

I’m not in Love or in Order or in anything but Pain 

 

Pain – the Sichuan fire-breather breaks and enters

I’d prefer Pace in retrospect – and why’d I spurn Scale

Pain – the Sichuan fire-breather breaks and enters

 

Pain has brought

Fireworks and knives

Some scars which

Could or could not heal

 

A whirlwind, and she is gone

 

Pain – the Sichuan fire-breather – I’m broken

 

And so I sit

Wishing for when

I cursed Pace’s name

When I sat next to Scale

 

Today, I’ve been poked and prodded and made to know who and what I am

That I’m real

I will learn to appreciate all the phenomenological spirits in time

Until then I sit and watch as they run rampant through the city

Except Scale – that beautiful fun house mirror

I sit and look up waiting for her to whisper in my ear
