Open/close

I open to the world and then have to close.

I have always hated the phrase, “you think too much.”

And I don’t hate much in this world

 

What, I hear is:

 

Stop being so fully yourself.  We feel uncomfortable.  

I can’t or won’t meet you where you are.

So I close.

 

I know we all guard ourselves, but why these closures?

Can’t we talk in layers and nuance and metaphor?

And can I analyze multiple angles without that bite?

 

You think too much – you care too much

 

Let me know you’re tired or it’s not a good time

Ask if we can talk about something lighter or...

Even just ask to sit in silence for a moment or two

 

This I understand - this is communication

This I can work with and follow and still be me

This doesn’t cut down who I am – how I see

 

You think too much – you care too much

 

How could I do anything but close down

They say systems should be open for extension

But closed for modification – open yet closed

Are we not systems?  Seems a SOLID practice to me

 

Let me extend, bounce, explore—

Without the threat of being written over

I’m not attempting to change you

And so respect me enough to do the same

You think too much – you care too much


No, I don’t
