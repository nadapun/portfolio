Spilled Noodles

 

Into a building none of us live in

The elevator up to the umpteenth floor

Left then right... no, left then left then right

Breach a door which should be locked

Teamwork up a crumbled wall

Pass the bags, pass the beers

Scramble across an awkward gap

 

And the city explodes

Kings would kill for this view, so this morning I am a king

 

There before the sun crests

A group of strangers – all I’ve just met

A bizarre crew admiring a helluva view

There’s nowhere I’d rather be

A few looks down, a few cracked beers

Eight kuai noodles are passed around

We sit and we wonder

Alive if for now

And then the smallest of blunders

 

Spilled Noodles

Our mark on the roof already being eyed by the birds

 

An artist at heart – an opportunity

Spilled anything is spilled ink

So I plop down beside the mess

And begin to assess

The possibilities presented to me

My new friends dancing and singing

Reveling in the joy of the sun

A bird even comes down to help me

Rearrange these dan dan mian.

 

The simplest of faces

It’s all that I made – by rearranging the mess I had caused

 

I pay for these moments of course

I should be more careful and calm

But a life without such moments

Is not one I wish to claim

I could be more healthy and thoughtful

More charitable, more modest, more humble

But if I didn’t step into these moments

The world would have less art - less me

 

Only for a moment

Chengdu Face – Spilled Noodles – Andy existed somewhere

 

So, I’m grateful for sleep when I get it

For regularity and slow pace and calm

But I also celebrate my spontaneity, my vision

Companions for a night, friendly birds,

Breathtaking views, and dancing on roofs

These moments may not define me

But they bring me a peace thinking back

The price of spilled anything means nothing

If you allow yourself to see it as art

So after that day I’m spilled on my bed

Regretting my choices for sure

But spilled anything is spilled ink...

So all I’ve gotta do is plop down beside me

Assess the mess and change a few things

And just label myself as art
