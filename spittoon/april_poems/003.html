Field of Forgotten Dreams

 

Lying in a field of forgotten dreams

No place to be, the sunlight’s not a threat

More an invitation back to that phantasmagorical world

Where time and space stand bereft

 

Take me back to that edge of sleep

Where my mind can dance through the cosmos

Where color is music, and music is shape, and I’ve lost my shape

Without those should’ve’s and ought-to’s

 

And then back to the field again

Relearning my place in the world – who I am

Rolling over in my sheets, wrapped in memories of a different world

Smiling as I remember who I am

 

But then back down, ego gone

The Universe and reality split at the seams

Reveling as light explodes and wanes, grows and reveals me

Back in the field of forgotten dreams
