Seeds Suck - Succeed

 

Each poem – each piece of art needs a seed.

I will concede pre-seed I don’t know how to proceed

Some artists seem able to work pre-seed

As if they are con-seed – though not conceited

If you think seeds suck, how can you succeed?

I am very much pro-seed before proceeding

Preceding a seed, I must recede within myself

Emerging with a seed, hoping to exceed the last seed

But when the limits of the idea are exceeded, re-recede

And re-seed, the ex-seed can be visited again

But only after taking a new perspective – no pun
