Knee Deep

 

Knee deep

Muk – mire

Sludge

Walking through a corral after a hard rain

 

But I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

I can see half way to my front door

But knee-deep, clinging, rotting viscera

Blankets my hallway – spreads past my sight

 

But I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

I stand up

Muk – mire

Sludge

I could return to the comfort of my bed

 

But I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

Yesterday’s demons slain don’t simply go away

They’re festering on my floor – I sink in each step

Lifting my next foot a struggle as my resolve wavers

 

But I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

I shower

Muk – mire

Sludge

It begins to wash away – simple tasks

 

I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

As I dress, the knee-deep viscera gives me a reminder

You could stay - The world out there’s darker

Than the knee-deep death in here... I shake it off

 

I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

I pack my bag

Muk – mire

Sludge

Clings to each device – more weight

 

But I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

I make it to my door with everything I need

A sword of music, A notebook as my shield

And I open the door – now was that so hard?

 

I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face

 

Call my car

No muk

No mire

I’m out to slay some demons

 

Tomorrow the slime might be a simple coating

Tomorrow the deluge could crawl up the walls

Tomorrow if it’s knee-deep, I can make the walk

 

For I’ve faced many demons and I have more to face
