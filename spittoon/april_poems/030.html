Tomorrow

 

Tomorrow, I see light

Will I write?

Will I fight

Against every prompt?

 

Tomorrow, I roll over

No more prompts

No more impetus

No more direction - did I have a reason?

 

Tomorrow, I pack my bag

Will I write again?

What do I pack?

Pens and paper or an umbrella?

 

Tomorrow, I will study

Not poetry

Not verse

Code and ethics - not far behind

 

Tomorrow, I sleep

Overloaded

Work - poems - study

May comes with flowers and rest

 

Tomorrow, I ask

Was it worth it?

Diligent to a fault

Did I prove anything to myself - or you?

 

Tomorrow, I reset

I did the thing

Posted daily and why?

Am I a better person for doing so?

 

Tomorrow, I relax

I did it - I did it

I don't know why

Not a poet but something else

 

Tomorrow, I wonder

Was it worth it?

Am I a poet?

Is persistence more powerful than talent?

 

Tomorrow, I drink

I mean I had to

Exposing myself

My vices an excuse for my kindness or vice-versa

 

Tomorrow, I lie

Protecting you or me?

Not malicious

I want it to just be okay

 

Tomorrow, I breathe

Lucky lucky

I am so lucky

Forget all this quiet sand falling through my hands

 

Tomorrow, I see

Not light - not brilliance

Quiet kindness in the dark

I just want to help - to be here

 

Tomorrow...

What am I tomorrow?

Will I write?

Will I fight?

Does any of that even matter?

Do I hold the trump card of kindness?

Am I just too weak to take action?

Tomorrow, will I sit?

Tomorrow, will I grow?

Tomorrow, will I write a poem which reaches no one?

Tomorrow, will I change the world?

Tomorrow, will I be changed by the world?

Tomorrow, will I cry?

Tomorrow, will I let simple unadorned niceties control how I view the world?

Tomorrow, will I make a kind joke?

Tomorrow, will I fall in love?

Tomorrow, will I make art?

Tomorrow, will I make someone better?

Tomorrow, will I make me better?

Tomorrow, will I be better?

Tomorrow, will I sit?

Tomorrow, will I grow?

 

Tomorrow -

Will I write a poem?
