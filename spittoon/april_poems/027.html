Unconditional Love

 

i gave you my unconditional love

but i am guarded

is guarded a condition?

scared to open past wounds

so i guess i gave you my conditional love

 

i gave you my unconditional love

but then i saved you

and it took me awhile to heal

i still love you but needed space

so i guess i gave you my conditional love

 

i gave you my unconditional love

no questions asked

and you took advantage of me

so i climbed inside my shell and you left

so i guess i gave you my conditional love

 

i give you my unconditional love

but you move so fast

i can’t keep up – too far away to know

i think i still love you and you me

so i guess our love is conditional

 

i love unconditionally too often...

and then it is conditionally

for i don't know how oft i've been loved unconditionally

 

but i will still give you my unconditional love

not closed, not shut down

listening - showing up - reciprocating

perhaps my love scares others

or perhaps i am insecure and broken

and shut down too easily

has all my love been conditional?

 

forget the past

step forward

i give you my unconditional love
