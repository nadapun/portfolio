Lacking

 

Today I lack patience - I lack calm

I lack the mental energy to write a poem

I lack the I don’t know... but today

I lack some sort of clarity

Pushed down to a lack because of

No one stepping up, stalled promises

Today I am lacking

 

A habit born of repetition

Open m’phone in the car for study

And a strange reminder as I review

A grammar flash card profound

 

你之所以能够追求精神上的快乐，

是因为你已经有了稳定的物质基础

 

And suddenly I realize I am not lacking

I have food, I have shelter, I have security

I have been lucky enough to have...

The mental energy to write a poem a day

The creativity to build and paint and code

The philosophical space to ask questions

 

So, I don't lack time, I shouldn't lack calm

I am lucky to dance through this world

Writing of bees and drawing strangers

I merely lack the constant presence of mind

That the only thing I lack at times is

Appreciation.
