Freestyle

 

Freestyle – no form

Dancing with Chaos

Releasing the bounds

Order, a friend left at the door?

 

Wondering does

meter and rhyme

kill the sublime

 

Order – a book of chess maneuvers  

 

locked in this cage – lost my divinity

a profane man left to profanity

 

Is our problem

Taking any good and imprecise idea

And forming a discipline?

Losing the mirror of the idea

Replacing it with rigor

Rules

 

And so I embrace Chaos – spilled ink

I dance with him and seek council

Climbing mountains, asking questions

Seeking release, yet still bound

 

Order – the ink bottle, letters, language

He has lessons to impart, no doubt

But left with only style - no freedom

Nothing is freestyle

 

And so i ponder

Wiser is the man who knows Order,

Or he who deftly dances with Chaos?

 

I sit on the roof’s edge, admiring an endless view

And a visit from a friend

As Scale — that elusive fun-house mirror —

Sits down beside me,

Cross-legged and quiet,

And whispers in my ear

Chaos and Order wandering below

no answers

just a better question
