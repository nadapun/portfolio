Eldritch but sexy

 

I’ve heard the voice eight times - Eldritch but sexy

 

Walking through the Jewel Basin... admiring the view

And beside me a shadow asks, “Beautiful, huh?”

Five foot four, perhaps South American, straight shoulder length hair

But I can see straight through her, the trees and branches

texture her beautiful form – all the more intriguing

 

I can’t answer

 

I sit on a log and she/it does too, “Are you real?” I ask

A giggle is all I get in response – She/it looks forward

The view certainly is one to behold, we stare awhile

“How did you find this place?” she/it asks and I freeze

 

Should I be here? Is she real? Am I just tired from hiking?

It’s Joe’s secret spot – he showed me last summer

Innocent enough

 

“Do you know Joe?” - another giggle

Oh no, Is Joe okay?

“Is Joe okay?” I ask - another giggle, but then a response

“If I pushed you down this ridge, no one would ever find you.”

 

Fucking fuck that but I gotta chill

“Did you push Joe?”

“I miss Joe,” she/it replies

“Did you push him?”

“No, but I could’ve”

“You’re lucky...”

“Why?”

“I can’t push you until you push Joe.”

I’m absolutely terrified – frozen for minutes

We sit and admire the view – I reach out to touch her but she stands in a blink

I resolve myself to death – the beauty of the locale can’t prevent my heart’s sink

One hour, ten – I don’t know

Her/its presence is intoxicating without conversation

I stand to leave

 

“Am I going to kill Joe?”

A giggle

 

I walk out... easiest hike of my life

Didn’t need the trail mix, the water

Didn’t need a break

No sounds of birds, but I don’ t feel watched

 

I am home, Joe is fine

“Have you seen her/it?”

“What? Dude, settle.”

“Jewel Basin, looking over Twin Lakes,

the spot we went last summer.”

“Awesome trip, right?

wanna go again this summer?”

“Dude... what is she?”
“What is who?”
“Nevermind”

 

Is she even real?

Seven times since the first

Sometimes she is there

Sometimes it’s just a whisper

Sometimes she/it’s in my home

Once she/it was playing my piano

A Chopin Nocturne

“Would you like music when it’s time?”

“Please leave.”

“Not a fan of Chopin?”

A quick transition to Ravel

More beautiful than I’ve ever heard

“Just go hiking and shove him in a lake.”

 

I cannot... she/it’s not real

 

I retreat to my room and she/it walks through the wall

 

“I know you. Would you like to lay down.

You seem lonely.”

 

“No”

She/it seems to see my resolve and fades out of the wall pretending a stride.

 

6 more times... work, school, dreams – once on the subway

And the same message each time... Be with me, I can kill you once for us if you kill Joe

 

Joe wants to go camping with me up in Jewel Basin... and I’m losing it a bit.
