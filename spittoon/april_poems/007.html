Green Thumb

 

sometimes i feel gifted – green-thumbed

not now

天赋的天府的老外

不现在

 

Pen is on the page

But nothing’s taking root

Scribbles pushing dirt

 

The view's no help, today

Called Chengdu my city

可是我不是本地

 

As a man of the earth

On foreign soil, some days

I'm fighting through a haze

 

Wishing for an idea

a sprout, a bud, a seed

perhaps this is simply greed

 

明天再继续

today i will succumb

even the tianfu

sometimes lose their green thumb
